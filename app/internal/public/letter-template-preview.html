Appendix A: Relevant Legislation

The Goods Vehicles (Licensing of Operators) Act 1995 sets out the requirements for holding an operator's licence. Section 26 provides that the Traffic Commissioner may take regulatory action where an operator fails to comply with the conditions of their licence.

Appendix B: Your Rights

You have the right to make written representations in response to this letter. If a public inquiry is called, you will be given the opportunity to attend and present your case in person or through a legal representative.
